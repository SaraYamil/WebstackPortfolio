Marrakech is known for its vibrant culture and entertainment scene, including dinner shows that offer a unique blend of Moroccan cuisine and traditional entertainment. One popular option is to experience a Moroccan dinner accompanied by live music, and other performances.

On the terrace or in the dining room, the dinners served at BeldiFood will awaken the taste buds of the finest gourmets. Among the selection of the most renowned places in Marrakech, BeldiFood stands out through its refined Moroccan cuisine. A unique setting that takes place on the terrace rather than in the dining room, BeldiFood invites you every evening to come and discover a trendy menu and attend the biggest artistic shows in the ocher city. Every evening (except Wednesday), the most beautiful voices of Morocco perform on stage to make your dinner a wonderful experience.

Every Wednesday, the live band gives way to Moroccan artists to bring guests the magic of the Orient. The restaurant offers you a buffet made up of the best of Moroccan gastronomy accompanied by a Moroccan folk orchestra and gnawas.

Cuisine: 100% Moroccan

Live band every day from 9:30 p.m. to 11:00 a.m. (except Wednesday).

Every Wednesday: Moroccan Evening
